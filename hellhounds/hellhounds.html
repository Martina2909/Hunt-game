  • STORY:
    Hellhounds are pack of animals, they are agressive and fast. They work together to take down a target.

  • They always hunt in packs ranging from 2 up to 5.

  • Hellhounds are extremely fast, so outrunning them is not an option.

  • They don't have any kind of special defense, but also no vulnerabilities either.
