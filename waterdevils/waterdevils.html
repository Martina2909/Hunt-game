  • STORY:
    Water Devil is a swarm of smaller aquatic animals, which seem to sense the presense of anything entering its home waters. Its movements are very quick and decisive.

  • Water Devil betrays its presence with a rippling effect in the water and a periodic clicking sound.

  • If the player doesn't leave the water immediately, the creature inflicts very high damage very rapidly.

  • Water Devil can be "killed" with 3-4 shots on avarage. However, he will soon respawn because it can never be permanently killed.
