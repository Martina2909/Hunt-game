  • STORY:
    The Hive is a woman, whose torso has been twisted and hijacked by host called Hive Mother. Its torso has erupted to allow expansion of the insects.

  • Causes poison damage, making it impossible to heal for its duration and halting automatic regeneration of partially damaged health chunks.

  • To take her down, headshot is the best option.

  • Swarms of insects can be outrun, but it takes a long time leaving the player exposed to the attack.
