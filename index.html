  • Hunt: Showdown is a multiplayer first-person shooter video game. Developer and publisher is Crytek. The game was released on 27 August 2019.

  • The game takes place in Louisiana's countrysides around 1890s, during a plague. It is believed that the plague and virus spreading was caused by an opening of a rift from another universe which caused a supernatural being called The Sculptor. The Sculptor made bosses and monsters.

  • In the game, you are a bounty hunter who kills mythical bosses and monsters to claim a bounty and survive long enough to reach an extraction point without getting killed by another player.

  • The goal is to collect as many clues as possible so that the bosses location is narrowed down.

  • Once the player collects the bounty, he is given an ability to see the approximate location of other nearby players. The catch is that other players can now also see your location.
