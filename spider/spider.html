  • STORY:
    Spider is a person who mutated into a spider-looking creature. It's a creature not quite human and not quite spider, a semi-sentient mass of limbs, poisonous in both body and intent.

  • Spider can have two attacks, one of them being pounce attack, which spider announces with a screeching before he jumps. The second attack is spit attack where he spits a poison cloud towards the player.

  • When Spider enters frenzy mode, he has a very high damage resistance and deals 25 damage by stabbing claws into the player.

  • Its strength is that he is immune to poison and is very fast and agile.

  • Its weakness is that it's weak to fire and blunt.
