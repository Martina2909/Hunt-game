  • STORY:
    Grunts are slow-moving and mostly human creatures and also victims of a viral infection.

  • There are many variants of Grunts. First one being Grunt with Cleaver. They attack with cleaver, causing player to bleed.

  • Second one is Grunt with Torch. They attack with torch, causing light burning.

  • Third one is Doctor Grunt. They deal poison damage with rusty bone saw. If killed, they drop a Medkit.

  • Fourth one is Miner Grunt. He can wield a torch or cleaver. If the headlamp is lit, gunshot result in fiery explosion. If the headlamp is unlit, attack to the head results in a pool of oil left on the ground.
