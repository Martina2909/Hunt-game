  • STORY:
    Butcher is a huge bloated body with bits of wood and metal protruding from the skin. He has a leather apron which is covering the horrors of its torso. He has a head of a pig and a flaming hook.

  • Butcher attacks the players by throwing fireballs towards the player and setting him on fire.

  • Butcher is weak to rending.

  • Eventhough Butcher has stronger attacks, he is easy and fast to kill when using an axe.

  • One of Butcher's attacks is running after the player with the same speed as the sprinting speed of the player while swinging his hook.
