  • STORY:
    Assassin is a tall humanoid figure who can seemingly melt into a swarn of insects.

  • Assassin deals damage by stabbing the player with its bone spears.

  • Assassin can make a clone out of himself, which explodes if near player.

  • Assassin is weak to poison and easily interruped when he leaves his bug form.

  • Assassin is hard to hit and takes marginally less damage in his swarm form.
